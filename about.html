About Lifeline Care Hospital

We are committed to delivering safe, ethical, and evidence-based care with empathy at every touchpoint.

Mission

To improve lives through accessible, high-quality healthcare rooted in compassion and innovation.

Vision

To be a trusted regional leader in advanced healthcare outcomes and patient-centered excellence.

Values

Integrity, empathy, transparency, safety, and continuous clinical improvement.

History & Achievements

Founded in 2001, Lifeline Care Hospital has expanded from a community clinic to a multi-specialty institution with advanced critical care, robotic diagnostics, and telehealth support. We have received awards for patient safety, nursing excellence, and emergency response quality.

Certifications & Accreditations

  • National Healthcare Quality Accreditation (NHQA)
  • ISO 9001 Certified Clinical Processes
  • Joint Commission-aligned Patient Safety Protocols
  • Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS) Certified Emergency Team
